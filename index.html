JustLend : The Future of Decentralized Perpetual Trading

Introduction to JustLend

JustLend is the first official lending protocol on the Tron blockchain, enabling users to supply and borrow digital assets in a fully decentralized, transparent, and non-custodial manner. As a key player in the DeFi ecosystem, JustLend empowers users to earn passive income by providing liquidity or to access instant crypto loans, all while retaining full control over their assets.

What is JustLend?

JustLend is a money market protocol where users can deposit TRX or TRC-20 tokens and receive interest, or use their crypto as collateral to borrow other assets. The protocol utilizes algorithmic interest rates, audited smart contracts, and a transparent on-chain governance system. JustLend is integrated with major wallets like Trust Wallet and TronLink, making DeFi lending accessible to everyone.

  • Decentralized lending and borrowing of TRX and TRC-20 tokens
  • Earn interest by supplying liquidity
  • Non-custodial—retain ownership of your assets at all times
  • Integration with top wallets including Trust Wallet
  • Transparent and audited smart contracts

Why JustLend Stands Out

  • High Yields: Competitive, real-time interest rates for lenders
  • Instant Liquidity: Borrow crypto instantly against your deposits
  • Security: Funds remain in your wallet, and the protocol is regularly audited
  • Governance: Participate in protocol decisions through on-chain voting

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Use JustLend with Trust Wallet

  1. Install Trust Wallet: Download Trust Wallet from the official app store and set up your wallet.
  2. Deposit TRX or Tokens: Add TRX or supported TRC-20 tokens to your Trust Wallet.
  3. Connect Wallet: Connect Trust Wallet to JustLend using the “Connect Wallet” button.
  4. Supply Assets: Choose the asset you want to supply, enter the amount, and confirm the transaction to start earning interest.
  5. Borrow (Optional): Use your supplied assets as collateral to borrow other tokens directly from the platform.
  6. Track and Manage: Use JustLend’s dashboard in Trust Wallet to monitor your balances, earned interest, and loans.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – JustLend

Is JustLend Safe?

Yes. JustLend’s smart contracts are audited, and funds always remain non-custodial in your wallet.

Which Wallets Can I Use?

Trust Wallet, TronLink, and Ledger are all compatible with JustLend’s lending and borrowing features.

How Do I Earn Interest?

By supplying assets to the protocol, you earn algorithmic interest that accrues in real time.

What Are the Fees?

There are no deposit or withdrawal fees on JustLend, but standard Tron network transaction fees apply.

Can I Withdraw Anytime?

Yes, you can withdraw supplied assets and earned interest at any time, subject to liquidity.

How Can I Track My Loans?

Use the JustLend dashboard in Trust Wallet or on the web to monitor all your loans, interest, and collateral.
